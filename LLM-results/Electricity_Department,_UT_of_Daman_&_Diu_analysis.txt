The electricity rates for residential customers in Daman and Diu are categorized as follows:

1. For 0-50 units: INR 1.60 per kWh
2. For 51-100 units: INR 1.60 per kWh
3. For 101-200 units: INR 2.30 per kWh
4. For 201-400 units: INR 2.80 per kWh
5. For 401 and above: INR 3.40 per kWh
6. For Life Line Consumers (up to 2x40 W bulbs only): INR 1.00 per kWh【4:0†Daman and Diu TarrifOrder.pdf】.

Regarding Electric Vehicle (EV) tariffs, they are as follows:

- LT Category: INR 5.10 per kVAh
- HT Category: INR 4.90 per kVAh【4:0†Daman and Diu TarrifOrder.pdf】.
